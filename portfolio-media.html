Illuminations

This piece was projected for two weeks onto a large white wall, in May 2006, as part of Threshold at Fesof, 88 Waybossett Providence, RI, USA. The work takes as a departure point the first lines of Rimbaud’s 'Illuminations'.

Blood of a Poet

Blood of a Poet: An interactive installation. Processed video & sound, projected into the center of a painting. The raw materials for the video were derived from Cocteau's original 1030's abstract film 'Le Sang d'un Poète'
