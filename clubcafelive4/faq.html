FAQ

Can my underage child attend a concert with me?

Unfortunately not, all concerts are strictly 21+.

Do you serve food during concerts?

Club Cafe serves food during all concerts except late night events.

Can I record, videotape, or photograph the show?

Flash photography, audio recording and video recording are strictly prohibited. Professional photography must be approved in advance.

What is the parking situation at Club Cafe?

Street parking is available (meters not enforced after 6pm). There is also an open air parking lot on E. Carson St. between 12th and 13th St. (enforcement hours posted in lot).

I am attending a concert, can I reserve seating?

Limited seating is available on a first-come, first-serve basis only. No reservations are taken.

I am disabled, can I reserve seating?

Yes, please call us at 412.431.4950 or email us at feedback@clubcafelive.com
